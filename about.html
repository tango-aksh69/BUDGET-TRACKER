About Our Budget Tracker

Our Budget Tracker is a simple yet powerful tool designed to help you manage your daily, weekly, or monthly expenses effortlessly. It allows you to create categories such as Food, Travel, or Entertainment, assign a budget to each, and then track your spending in real-time.

How to Use

  • Enter a category name and set a budget amount, then click Add Category.
  • Your total budget and all categories will appear on the screen.
  • Use the Spend section to record expenses — the remaining balance updates automatically.
  • If you spend more than the allocated amount, the value can go negative, indicating overspending.

Why You Should Use This

Budget Tracker helps you build financial awareness by keeping a close eye on your spending habits. Whether you're a student managing your allowance, a professional tracking daily expenses, or a family planning monthly budgets — this tool helps ensure you stay within limits and make smarter money decisions.
